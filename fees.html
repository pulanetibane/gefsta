Fees

The course is R24 600 which includes:

  • School fees:R18 000.00 - R9000 registration fee.
  • After paying registration, the balance of R9 000.00 is to be paid in instalments before the end of 3months (R3000x3 months).
  • Package: R3 500 to be paid on the day of arrival for Books and uniform.
  • First Aid: R3 100.00 to be paid before the exams.
  • Accommodation is FREE(self catering). If not satisfied, student is allowed to find his/her own accommodation.
