About Us

Overview

The Goldfields Emergency Fire Services Training Academy (GEFSTA) is a training academy that specializes in emergency services and industrial courses. It offers both on-site and off-site training to municipalities and companies. The academy is accredited by SAESI/IFSAC, DOL, HWSETA, and LG SETA, emphasizing practical training.

Courses we offer are Firefighter 1&2, Hazmat awareness, Fire Safety Awareness, First Aid level 1,2,3, Fire Services Intructor, Hazmat Operational course and Hazmat Operational Core.

Vision Statement

To develop approved training products and services by means of regular training needs of our existing as well as potential students and to satisfy their needs with acceptable training products and services.

Mission Statement

Provide training courses of the highest quality.
