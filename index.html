Welcome to Goldfields Emergency Fire Services Training Academy

The Goldfields Emergency Fire Training Academy (GEFSTA) is a training academy that specializes in emergency services and industrial courses. It offers both on-site and off-site training to municipalities and companies. The academy is accredited by SAESI/IFSAC, DOL, HWSETA, and LG SETA, emphasizing practical training.
